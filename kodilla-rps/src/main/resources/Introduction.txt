Welcome to Rock - Paper - Scissors - Lizard - Spock game!

This is an extended version of classic Rock - Paper - Scissors.
You can find full list of rules below:
1. Paper covers rock.
2. Rock crushes scissors.
3. Rock crushes lizard.
4. Spock vaporizes rock.
5. Scissors cut paper.
6. Lizard eats paper.
7. Paper disproves Spock.
8. Scissors decapitate lizard.
9. Spock smashes scissors.
10. Lizard poisons Spock.
11. Draw.

Controls:
1 - Choose rock
2 - Choose paper
3 - Choose scissors
4 - Choose lizard
5 - Choose Spock
x - End game
n - Start new game
